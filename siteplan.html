Motiv8 Daily - Website Plan

Site Name

Motiv8 Daily

I chose this name, Motiv8 Daily, because the site will share simple daily motivation and short advice. It sounds positive and easy to remember.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide daily motivational quotes, simple life advice, and short messages to help people stay focused and encouraged. The site will use JavaScript to show random quotes, save favorites to localStorage, and update messages daily.
